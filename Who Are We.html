Who We Are

YCC


Who we are? We are independent, non - profit, and voluntary organization based in Kokand, Uzbekistan. Our org is a coalition of young people and activists to take a positive action on climate crisis.









Our Mission

Our mission is to solve climate crisis, inspire the community to work together to save the planet and make a better life where clean water flows and diverse ecosystems thrive.
