Climate Change In Uzbekistan

There is no country that is not experiencing severe effects of climate change. With no exception Uzbekistan is one of them and is one of the particularly vulnerable countries to climate change. People in here haven't seen real snow in years. Anyhow, it can be limited with increased investment, today’s technology and with an immediate action.









Everyone Should Take Action



  1. Take public transit

  2. Buy organic and local whenever possible

  3. Don’t waste food, Grow your own

  4. Use energy wisely — and save money too!

  5. Start a climate conversation

  6. Consume less, waste less, enjoy life more
